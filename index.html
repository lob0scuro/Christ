Son of Man

In a small village in the middle east, circa 3 A.D. there was a young couple who were engaged to be married. Having no relations, the girl found herself with child. The man must have been heart broken, disgusted, irrate! and the girl... fearful, but at peace. Why? Later that night, Joseph had a dream. An angel of the Lord came to him and said

Joseph, son of David, do not be afraid to take Mary as your wife; for the Child who has been conceived in her is of the Holy Spirit. She will bear a son; and you shall call His name Jesus, for He will save the people from their sins.

Joseph had a choice. Dwell on the hurt, secretly send her away to avoid public shaming, or trust in God. Joseph chose to trust in Him and took Mary as his wife. Joseph and Mary traveled to Bethelehem (Joseph's town of origin) and found last minute lodging in a stable behind an Inn, just as Mary was giving birth. way up in the sky above them, a star broke out of the darkness and began to shine brighter than any of it's contemporaries. It was a sign of hope. But for some, it was a threat. Herod (then Ruler of Judea) inquired about the star and what it meant, and he wasn't pleased with the answer he got.
